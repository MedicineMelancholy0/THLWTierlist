841:

I think Gengetsu should be lower in EX. the combination of 3p break while having party 1p on resonance skill means she is almost always forced to toggle on skill 3 T1, making her support on last gauge extremely poor. Yes she can stack -8 def down with 50% card even when enemy is immune to burn/blind BUT her p0 killers are practically non-existent and the AoE has relatively low raw damage in general and does not have party p/barrier on AoE. Skill 2 is more difficult to save nowadays because her AoE pre-eff inflicts blind which is realistically only broken by Mima (who you don’t bring nowadays) and etoyo (on 3p and thus heavily limiting her AoE damage). Her support is decent between her skill 3, a Sun/light/party 4 Yin d card and a few buffs sprinkled here and there. However, the two 100% stored power units in EX have roughly similar, if not more support while still being able to AoE nuke occasionally without relying on eff. Cs2 gets to ignore her AoE damage issue because she has infinite party P, ridiculous amount of buffs, party 3 Yin ATK II for 3T and a few good cards to make up for it. Such are a luxury that Gengetsu doesn’t always get to afford. She is not bad by any means but her highs seem to be overestimated at the moment. Unless they delete all barriers from DS, she would be overshadowed by most of her colleagues in EX units who also has the means to be really amazing unlike Gengetsu who only has her consistency to boast in exchange of actual highs.

Cs2 is quite frankly a mid A tier if used in the damage role. Being a geng bot is simply that important inherently. Geng has a similar amount of buffs on average. Due to being able to take care of acc so they dont have to run an acc card. And otherwise the only difference is cs2 is just simply better in the last gauge and has a damage role as opposed to gengetsu have a very poor one. I dont get the complaint her aoe inflicts blind.,..just..break it? If youre aiming to not break it, by abusing def down, why have the complaint blind units are in a tough spot? Overall just..being a universal geng bot is simply that valuable these days. Her support is good enough to make it worth her going first due to not needing to take care of acc unlike cs2. And in general...being a geng is just that important tbh. Does it seem weird that despite being a worse cs2 (USUALLY) shes still where she is? Perhaps. But its just that valuable. For example. The stored power bots are great truly. But when have you required a stored power to make a real comp? You basically always require a geng equal in duos. So how does having stored power make one just as big of a requirement to comps? Just food for thought. Its a touchy and debatable subject in general.
